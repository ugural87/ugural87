# Uğur Alkan

Quantitative modeller — thirteen years of mathematical modelling across domains, from
continuum mechanics and constitutive modelling to financial markets. Currently completing
an MSc in Data Analytics (Sabancı University, 2026).

I build models the same way regardless of domain: explicit assumptions, leakage-free
protocols, calibrated probabilities, and an honest statement of what the data can and
cannot support. The unifying thread is that **models are not the deliverable — decisions
are.**

## Portfolio

**[portfolio_projects](https://github.com/ugural87/portfolio_projects)** — end-to-end

Current highlight: **Bank Customer Churn as a decision system** — six notebooks covering

calibration, a cost-matrix-derived campaign policy with sensitivity analysis, and a
propensity-score causal analysis of the activation lever.

In progress: credit risk (PD / IFRS 9 language), classical time series (SARIMA/GARCH),
deep learning forecasting (CNN + Transformer encoder), and an NLP/LLM track
(recommendation, sentiment, RAG).
